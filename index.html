Daniel Slonim



I am a doctoral student in mathematics at Purdue University, expecting to graduate with my PhD in spring 2022. After that I will be a postdoc at the University of Virginia. I study random walks in random environments, and am also interested in other areas of discrete probability, like percolation or random groth models. My advisor is Jonathon Peterson.
